Conclusions¶

The exploratory analysis has provided us with a lot of findings about the TMDB dataset. Let's see a summary of all them.¶

Findings : movie's popularity¶

Popularity is influential metric.For both the users and TMDb, users can determine watch that movie or not based on popularity rate. For TMDb , It helps them boost search results and more advantages.

So, my focus is mainly on the popularity. what is the most factors that effect movie's popularity? As you can see, There is a high positive correlation between popularity and number of voters. Then, vote average and release date. The most factors that effect movie's popularity is number of votes. While the number of users who votes for a movie increase , the popularity is also increased. which brings more and more votes. As result, It will add an incredible success chance for that movie.

To ensure of my findings, I search in 'The movie Database API' to know how is popularity for movies calculated. Here is what I came with : notice that there are some features are not included in our dataset

Findings : budget , revenue and vote average¶

The plot of budget and vote average show us that there is no correlation between them . It guide us to hypothesize that not all movies with high budget end up with higher TMDb vote. Same with revenue. There as a small impact of vote average on revenue. observations on the most right of revenue and vote average plot gives us an evidence for that.

Findings : most popular genre of movies¶

Top five of popular genres are in 'Drama', 'comedy' , 'Thriller' , 'Action' and 'Adventure'.'Drama' is the most popular genre of movies .

Limitations :¶

The dataset have some limits that impact analysis quality. The most important one is missing values. We have 10866 rows, after dropping rows with missing values (in our case is zero) we just only have 3728. If these movies were recorded , result of the analysis will be much accurate. Maybe the budget and revenue hava an impact on movie's success . Also, what if the popularity of these movies is influenced by other factors? The null values was 23 in genres, It is not that much but it would be helpful to have them. Moreover, There are some important factors are not included in dataset. They are related to our analysis , for example, the "vote_average" is calculated by the average of "Number of votes for the day" , "Number of total votes" and "Previous days score" . What if we have these factors in separated columns and the data is up to date? I think without these limitations the answers would be completely changed or completely accurate.
